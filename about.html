About Me

Last updated March 2026

I am an Embedded Systems Engineer with expertise in robotics, embedded software, and industrial automation. Currently at Moog Inc., I focus on software–hardware integration and DSP development.

I have always had a strong fascination with electronics and technology. My parents' old PC was my starting point — leading me through online forums, building my first computer at 13, and eventually earning a Master's degree in Robotics Engineering. I now use this enduring passion to propel innovation and tackle challenging problems in the engineering world.

Beyond engineering, a professor in high school introduced me to philosophy and literature, sparking a love of reading — from books and journals to philosophical papers. I've also participated in philosophy competitions addressing modern issues and existential questions. And I'm an avid hiker: the mountains give me perspective, adventure, and a closer bond with the natural world.
